Pasta Recipe

most average pasta recipe

after many lab tests we can confidently say , this pasta recipe is the most accurate completely average pasta ever made!!!

and you should be thankful! because with our clear and simple instructions, you will be able to make this perfectly average pasta yourself

ingredients:

  • 8 oz. penne pasta
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 yellow squash
  • 1 carrot
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup grape tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp Freshly cracked black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp Fresh lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp butter
